# Comprehensive Bibliography: German Language Acquisition, Dialects, and Registers

## Foundational Works

### German Linguistics General

- Durrell, M. (2017). *Hammer's German Grammar and Usage* (6th ed.). Routledge.
- Eisenberg, P. (2013). *Grundriss der deutschen Grammatik* (4th ed.). Metzler.
- Hentschel, E., & Weydt, H. (2013). *Handbuch der deutschen Grammatik* (4th ed.). De Gruyter.

### Historical Linguistics

- Wells, C. J. (1985). *German: A Linguistic History to 1945*. Clarendon Press.
- Salmons, J. C. (2012). *A History of German: What the Past Reveals about Today's Language*. Oxford University Press.
- Robinson, O. W. (1992). *Old English and Its Closest Relatives: A Survey of the Earliest Germanic Languages*. Stanford University Press.
